# 📊 Hotel Booking Analysis & Business Insights

## 🔹 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of hotel booking data using Power BI.  
The objective is to uncover meaningful insights related to booking trends, customer behavior, cancellations, revenue patterns, and operational efficiency.

The analysis is structured into multiple dashboards, enabling interactive exploration and data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Analyze booking trends and seasonal demand patterns  
- Understand customer segmentation and behavior  
- Identify key factors influencing cancellations  
- Evaluate pricing strategies using Average Daily Rate (ADR)  
- Assess operational efficiency and guest experience  
- Analyze distribution channels and market segment performance  

---

## 🧰 Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI  
- SQL (MySQL)  
- DAX  
- Excel  

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
- Booking details (dates, hotel type, lead time)  
- Customer information (type, number of guests)  
- Pricing details (ADR, meal plans)  
- Room allocation (reserved vs assigned)  
- Reservation status (Check-Out, Canceled, No-Show)  
- Distribution channels & market segments  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure
1. Executive Overview  
2. Booking Trends  
3. Customer Behavior  
4. Cancellation & Risk  
5. Revenue & Pricing  
6. Operations & Channels  
7. Guest Experience (Bonus)  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Strong seasonal booking trends  
- Transient customers dominate bookings  
- Cancellation varies across segments  
- ADR differs across hotels and meal plans  
- Efficient room allocation observed  
- Channels impact booking performance  

---
